Special Selection

Delicious Menu

  • Paneer Tikka

    Rs 250.50

    Cubes of Indian cottage cheese marinated in spiced yogurt, skewered, and grilled to perfection. This vegetarian delight offers a smoky flavor with a hint of tanginess.

  • Biryani

    Rs 200.00

    Aromatic basmati rice layered with succulent pieces of marinated meat (or vegetables), saffron, and a blend of traditional spices. Each mouthful is a celebration of flavor, served with cooling raita.

  • Rogan Josh

    Rs 600.00

    A Kashmiri delicacy, this dish features tender lamb or goat simmered in a fragrant gravy made with yogurt, garlic, and a blend of spices. The vibrant red color comes from Kashmiri chilies, offering a mild yet flavorful experience.

  • Chole Bhature

    Rs 40.00

    Spicy chickpea curry served with large, fluffy fried bread (bhature). A hearty and popular North Indian dish.

  • Palak Paneer

    Rs 250.00

    A smooth and vibrant spinach curry combined with cubes of paneer, lightly spiced to perfection.

  • Tandoori Chicken

    Rs 490.00

    Chicken marinated in yogurt and a special blend of spices, then grilled in a tandoor (clay oven) for a smoky, charred flavor.
